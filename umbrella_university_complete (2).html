Our Journey

27 Years of Milestones

From a single building with 120 students to a 200-acre university — every chapter has been written with purpose.

1998
Founded by Dr. M.M. Yadav
Umbrella College of Engineering opens its doors with 120 students across 3 departments — CSE, ECE, and Mechanical Engineering — on a rented 12-acre campus in the heart of the city.
2003
First Batch Placed at Infosys & TCS
Our inaugural batch of 118 graduates achieves 100% placement — 78% at top-tier companies including Infosys, TCS, and Wipro — setting a standard we've exceeded every year since.
2008
University Status Granted
The UGC grants Umbrella deemed university status. We expand to 200 acres, launch 6 new departments, and begin the journey from regional college to national institution.
2013
Research Centres Established
Three dedicated research centres open — AI & Cognitive Systems, Advanced Energy Lab, and the BioEngineering Institute — with ₹40Cr+ in initial government and industry funding.
2018
NAAC 'A+' Accreditation
Umbrella earns NAAC A+ status and enters the NIRF Top 25 for Engineering institutions. International partnerships with MIT, NTU Singapore, and TU Munich formalised.
2021
Smart Campus Initiative Launched
Umbrella becomes one of India's first fully IoT-connected campuses — smart classrooms, energy management AI, and a digital twin of the entire campus deployed in collaboration with Siemens.
2024
NAAC 'A++' & NIRF #12
Our highest-ever NIRF ranking (#12 nationally) and NAAC A++ grade mark the beginning of Umbrella's next chapter — a world-ranked research university by 2030.

FAQs

Common Questions

What is the eligibility for B.Tech admission? +

Minimum 60% in Class 12 with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics. JEE Main score accepted; our own entrance exam (USAT) is also available for students who haven't appeared for JEE.

Is hostel accommodation guaranteed? +

Yes — all first-year students are guaranteed hostel accommodation. Allocation is based on distance from home. From Year 2, students may choose to live off-campus.

Can I change my branch after Year 1? +

Yes. Branch change is allowed after Semester 2 based on CGPA rank. CSE and AI/ML branches require a minimum CGPA of 9.0. Up to 10% of seats per department are available for lateral transfer.

Are there education loan tie-ups? +

Yes — we have MoUs with SBI, HDFC Credila, and Avanse for education loans at preferential rates (starting 8.5% p.a.). A dedicated finance counsellor assists students with the application process.
